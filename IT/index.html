About the Website:

This website helps you detect if you might have diabetic retinopathy – a complication of diabetes – using artificial intelligence. All you need to do is upload an image of your eye (retina), and the system will quickly analyze it to give you an initial result that helps you better understand your condition. Our goal is to raise awareness and support early detection to help you protect your vision.
